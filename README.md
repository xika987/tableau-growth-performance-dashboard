# tableau-growth-performance-dashboard
This project is an anonymized business intelligence dashboard built to monitor acquisition performance and marketing efficiency across multiple markets. It consolidates key KPIs, highlights weekly trends, and supports data-driven decisions on budget allocation and market prioritization.
## Goals
- Provide a high-level KPI scorecard for business performance tracking
- Monitor efficiency and cost trends over time (weekly)
- Evaluate the relationship between spend and quality metrics (conversion)
- Compare investment strategy across market tiers (Core vs Growth)

## Key Metrics
- Total New Members
- Total Sign-ups
- Total Spend
- Conversion Rate (Sign-up â†’ Member)
- Cost per Member (efficiency)

## Dashboard Components
### 1) Top-Level Scorecards
Quick snapshot of core KPIs to track performance at a glance.

### 2) Weekly Efficiency Comparison
Trend view comparing Cost per Member and Conversion Rate across markets to detect performance shifts early.

### 3) Spend vs. Quality Correlation
Explores how spend levels correlate with conversion quality over time, helping identify diminishing returns or spikes.

### 4) Market Tier Strategy (Core vs Growth)
Shows budget allocation split between Core and Growth markets to support portfolio-level planning and prioritization.

## Tools Used
- Tableau (dashboard design & interactivity)
- Excel / Data preparation (cleaning, formatting, calculated fields)

## Notes on Data
The dataset and labels are anonymized and adjusted for confidentiality. The dashboard structure and analysis logic reflect real-world performance reporting needs.
